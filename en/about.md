## About Me

A product engineer based in Hangzhou, building easy-to-use tools to simplify life. I know a bit of Swift, Rust, Python, and design. I love open source, believe in AI, and invest in AI. Not into the tech-influencer scene, and a strong believer in long-termism. I write about tech and life on [**Tw93**](https://tw93.fun/) and share cool finds every week via [**Trend Weekly**](https://weekly.tw93.fun/).
