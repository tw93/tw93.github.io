### Personnel Supply and Demand

- Many people for limited positions.
- Graduate numbers increased from 350k/year to nearly 1.7M in 2022 (over 10M total). 73.8% failure rate for grad school entrance exams.
- Civil service was the "end of the universe," but salaries are being adjusted downward and bonuses suspended.
- A joke: In 2022, applicants for teacher certifications exceeded the birth rate.
