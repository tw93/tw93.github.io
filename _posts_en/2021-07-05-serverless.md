Surprisingly, I won the "Star Lecturer" on the spot and also met many friends. It was a very good experience. The sharing PPT can be seen [here](https://gw.alipayobjects.com/os/k/gmtc/GMTC.pdf). Looking forward to some input for you reading, and welcome friendly exchanges.
